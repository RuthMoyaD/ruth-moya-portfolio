The Effect of Interest Rates and Global Inflation on Cryptocurrencies and Tech Stocks (2018–2024)

Macroeconomic and financial analysis in Power BI using public data (FED, FRED, World Bank, Investing.com).

Bitcoin QQQ / Nasdaq-100 FED Rates Global Inflation

Context and Objective of the Analysis

Context

This dashboard explores the relationship between interest rates, inflation, and financial markets between 2018 and 2024. The main focus is on U.S. data (a global benchmark due to the dollar’s influence on the world economy), complemented by global inflation indicators from the World Bank for a broader macroeconomic perspective.

Objective

To understand how decisions by the Federal Reserve (FED) affect the behavior of risk assets, especially cryptocurrencies (Bitcoin) and technology stocks (ETF Nasdaq-100, QQQ). Turning points between monetary policy, inflation, and investment in digital assets are analyzed.
